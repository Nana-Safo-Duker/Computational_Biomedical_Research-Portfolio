## Background
This post summarizes current research directions, methods, evidence quality, and translational implications.

## Methods and Evidence
Recent studies combine feature engineering, machine learning, and robust validation to improve reliability.

## Findings
Performance gains are most credible when externally validated and clinically contextualized.

## Limitations
